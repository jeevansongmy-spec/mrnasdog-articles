> Originally published at **[mrnasdog.com/analysis/coin-alerts](https://mrnasdog.com/analysis/coin-alerts)** by MrNasdog.

Set it once in your profile. When my researched number moves, you get one short email. No spam, no noise.

## What this is

Every coin page on this site carries one number I care about most: the **Next-90d inflation** — how much new supply I expect that coin to add (or burn) over the next 90 days, based on my own research.

That number only changes when I rebuild a coin's research and the facts have actually moved — a new unlock, a bigger burn, a changed emission schedule.

Coin alerts watch that number for you. You pick the coins you hold or follow. When one of them moves more than **0.5 percentage points**, I send you one short email showing the old number, the new number, and a link to the full research. That's the whole feature.

## Why this is different from price alerts

Every exchange can ping you when a price moves. Price moves all day — most of it is noise.

This alert fires on something slower and rarer: **the supply outlook itself changing.** It doesn't drift on its own, and it doesn't follow sentiment. When this number moves, something real changed in how the coin mints or burns. Some weeks you'll get nothing at all — that's by design. **No news is also information.**

## How to set it up (about 30 seconds)

Log in and open your [Profile](https://mrnasdog.com/profile) page. You'll find a card called **Coin alerts**:

![The Coin alerts picker on the profile page: a searchable coin list sorted by market cap, each row showing the coin's current Next-90d inflation reading.](https://mrnasdog.com/analysis/coin-alerts/picker.png)

Then three steps:

1. **Search** a coin (or scroll the list — coins are sorted by market cap).
2. **Tick** the coins you want to watch. The number next to each coin is its current Next-90d reading — green means supply is shrinking, red means it's growing.
3. Press **Save alerts**. Done.

![The picker with coins ticked and the Save alerts button ready.](https://mrnasdog.com/analysis/coin-alerts/picked-save.png)

You can come back and change your coins anytime.

## What the email looks like

When a coin you watch moves, you get one email like this — old number, new number, link to the research:

![The coin-alert email showing a coin's old Next-90d number, its new number, and a link through to the full research page.](https://mrnasdog.com/analysis/coin-alerts/email.png)

One email per day at most, covering all your coins that moved. And if you ever want out, there's a one-click "Turn off alerts" link in every email.

## One honest note

This alert covers **the supply side only**. It doesn't know about narrative, sentiment, or price — and a shrinking supply alone doesn't make a coin go up. Treat every alert as a reason to read the research, not a signal to act.
