## The verdict, in one paragraph

For the window opening July 2026, the MrNasdog Pressure Framework reads **XPL at about +74% net** on the forward view, driven almost entirely by the **Sep 25 2026** vesting cliff with no offset on the buy side. Our supply monitor reads the realized last-90-day change at **+9.53%**, versus the framework's **+10.26%** read for the same window — a gap of about **0.7 percentage points**, outside the 0.5-point tolerance, so a **monitor-gap chip is raised**. The gap is mechanism-driven: the framework books the full scheduled ecosystem release of about **267M** XPL, while the monitor sees on-chain circulating rise about **226M** — the difference is unlocked ecosystem supply still parked in the foundation treasury, not yet on the float. Plasma is **structurally inflationary on a heavy vesting overhang**: a young chain where most of the 10B genesis supply is still waiting to unlock.

## Sell pressure: where new XPL comes from

Sell #1 — protocol inflation — is **zero** today. Plasma's validator and staking rewards are not switched on yet; the documented **5%-to-3%** annual inflation schedule activates only once external validators and stake delegation go live, so total supply has not grown by a single token since the September 2025 genesis mint of **10B XPL**. Every bit of supply growth so far is reclassification, not issuance.

Sell #2 — vesting unlocks — is the headline, at about **1,665M XPL** in this window. The team allocation (**2.5B**) and the investor allocation (**2.5B**) each carry a one-year cliff from the September 2025 mainnet beta, and both release their first third — roughly **832.5M** each — on **Sep 25 2026**, squarely inside the next 90 days. That single cliff is the biggest supply event of XPL's year. A separate US public-sale tranche unlocks on **Jul 28 2026** as its 12-month lockup ends, but that supply is already counted in circulating, so it becomes tradable rather than net-new and does not add to the inflation figure.

Sell #3 — Foundation and unscheduled unlocks — is about **266.7M XPL** over 90 days. The Ecosystem & Growth allocation (**4B**, the largest bucket) releases roughly **88.9M** XPL a month to the foundation treasury through September 2028, deployed to grants, liquidity and partners; three monthly tranches fall in the window. Sell #4 — long-term locked or bankruptcy — is **zero**: Plasma launched in September 2025, so there is no bankruptcy estate and no court-ordered distribution.

## Buy pressure: where new XPL goes

Every buy row is **zero**, and that is what makes the cliff so heavy. Buy #1 — programmatic buyback — is zero: Plasma runs no token buyback and does not repurchase XPL from the market. Buy #2 — protocol fee burn — is effectively zero: an EIP-1559 base-fee burn exists, but stablecoin transfers on Plasma are **fee-sponsored** through a paymaster, so almost no base fee is burned, and what little is burned is immaterial against roughly **1.9B** of unlocking supply. Buy #3 — Foundation buy — is zero, with no open-market buying by the team or foundation in the window. Buy #4 — new long-term lock — is zero, with no fresh lockup or staking-lock programme carrying an announced quantum. With nothing on the buy side, the unlocks flow straight through to net inflation.

## Foundation and overhang

XPL is defined by its overhang: only about **2.6B** of the **10B** genesis supply circulates today, and nearly all of the rest is team-controlled or investor-locked. The **Ecosystem & Growth** reserve (~4B) is the active source, releasing about 88.9M a month into the foundation treasury on a published schedule that runs to September 2028. The **Team** allocation (2.5B) and the **Investor** allocation (2.5B) sit locked until their Sep 25 2026 cliff, then vest monthly for two more years — together they are the dominant future dilution. The framework re-checks the vesting calendar and the treasury balance on a roughly bi-weekly walk; if the ecosystem treasury's balance falls between refreshes as tokens move to the float, that outflow enters Sell #3 at the next refresh, and the September cliff is already booked in Sell #2.

## How XPL compares to other unlock-heavy Layer 1s

XPL belongs to the class of **young, low-float Layer 1s** — chains that launched with a small circulating slice of a large genesis supply and grow through scheduled team, investor and ecosystem unlocks rather than through block issuance. Structurally this is the opposite of a capped, halving-model chain like Bitcoin, where supply growth only slows over time and there is a hard ceiling. It is also unlike an exchange token that funds a quarterly buyback-and-burn from fee revenue: those tokens have a real buy-side mechanism leaning against issuance, while Plasma pays out no buyback and burns almost nothing on a fee-sponsored network.

The closest analogues are other **2024–2025 vintage L1s** with 12-month investor cliffs and multi-year linear vesting — the pattern where a single cliff date can add a double-digit percentage to the float overnight. What sets Plasma apart within that group is that its staking inflation is not even live yet, so today the entire inflation story is **vesting reclassification**, not emission. That makes the reading unusually front-loaded: quiet until a cliff, then a large step. Once validator rewards switch on, a second, continuous source of supply growth stacks on top of the unlock schedule — worth watching, but not yet in the numbers.

## What to watch in the next 90 days

The dated event that dominates everything is the **Sep 25 2026** team and investor cliff — about 1,665M XPL, the single largest release of the year, and the reason the forward read is so high. Watch the **Jul 28 2026** US public-sale unlock; it is already in circulating, but it widens the freely-tradable float. Watch the monthly ecosystem tranches on the 25th of each month (~88.9M each) and whether the foundation treasury deploys them to the market or holds them. Watch for any announcement that **staking and validator inflation** is going live, which would add a continuous 5%-and-decaying emission on top of the unlocks. And watch governance or tokenomics updates that could alter the vesting curve.
