My portfolios have always been public. Now you don't have to check them — the day I move real money, members get one short email.

## What this is

I run **four public portfolios** on this site with real money, and every trade I make lands on the [portfolio page](https://mrnasdog.com/portfolio) — that has been true from day one.

The missing piece: you had to come back and check. If I sold something on a Tuesday, you might not see it until the weekend.

**Portfolio alerts** close that gap. On any day I buy or sell in **any** of the four portfolios, members get **one email** listing every trade — what I bought or sold, how much, in which portfolio.

One digest per day, maximum. On days I don't trade — most days — **you get nothing.** Silence means nothing moved. I hate noisy inboxes as much as you do.

## How to control it (about 10 seconds)

If you're a member, there's nothing to set up — **it's already on.** To see it, or to turn it off:

1. **Step 1** — log in and open your [Profile](https://mrnasdog.com/profile).
2. **Step 2** — find the **"Your emails"** panel. Every email I ever send you lives there.
3. **Step 3** — the **Portfolio alerts** switch. Amber = on. Tap it off any time; tap it back on any time.

![The "Your emails" panel on the profile page: Weekly email toggle, Portfolio alerts toggle, and the Coin alerts picker — each its own card with an on/off switch.](https://mrnasdog.com/analysis/portfolio-alerts/emails-panel.png)

## Your profile is now one control panel

While building this, I rebuilt the profile page around a simple idea: **everything I might email you should be one switch, in one place.**

- **Weekly email** — the letter. On or off, one tap. No more hunting for an unsubscribe link.
- **Portfolio alerts** — this new one. My trades, the day they happen.
- **Coin alerts** — pick coins to watch; I email you when a coin's researched supply outlook changes. [Full write-up here](https://mrnasdog.com/analysis/coin-alerts).

You decide what reaches your inbox. Turn everything off and stay a member — the site still works. Turn everything on and you'll still get at most a couple of emails a week. That's the deal.

## One honest note

The email can arrive up to a day after the trade — it's a **daily digest by design**, because one calm email beats five pings. And these are **my own positions, shared for transparency** — not a signal to copy. I've been wrong before and I'll be wrong again; the email tells you *what I did*, and the research tells you *why*. Read the why.
